## Overview

Cycles are fixed time windows (sprints) that help a team focus on a slice of
work. Issues assigned to the active cycle show up in the team's cycle view,
with progress tracked as issues move through the workflow.

![](/img/docs/cycles.png)

## Enable cycles

Cycles are opt-in per team:

1. Go to **Settings → your team → Overview**.
2. Under **Team preferences**, enable cycles.

Once enabled, a **Cycles** entry appears in the team's sidebar and issues gain
a cycle property.

## Working with cycles

- **Assign issues:** Set the cycle from the issue's right-side properties
  panel, or in bulk from the list/board views.
- **Track progress:** The active cycle view groups its issues by status, so
  you can see remaining work at a glance.
- **Transitions:** Automated cycle transitions (closing a finished cycle and
  rolling unfinished issues into the next one) run as a background job and
  require [trigger.dev](/docs/oss/self-deployment#background-jobs-triggerdev)
  to be configured. Without it, you manage cycle boundaries manually.
